Why not "Login with EVE Online"?

Because many pilots have multiple accounts/characters to their name, Tech 3 wanted to create a system that treats pilots per player, not per account. Once your Tech 3 account has been created, you can begin adding all of your characters to your account and manage them from one place, without having to sign out to switch.
